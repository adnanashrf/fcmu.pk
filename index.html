SERVICES

We address your concerns, offering resolution, counseling, guidance, and collaborative support for comprehensive assistance tailored to your needs and well-being.

WOMEN COMPLAINT DESK

Women's protection and complaint desks are set up to create a safe and supportive place for women. Here, women can report and get help if they face any kind of harassment or violence.

ORPHAN & CHILD PROTECTION DESK

The desk's purpose is to guarantee the safety, well-being, and proper care for vulnerable children, including orphans, through well-coordinated support and necessary intervention.

OVERSEAS SERVICES DESK

The desk facilitates guidance and aid for various international endeavors, addressing inquiries, concerns, and providing resources to ensure smoother global experiences.

GENERAL SERVICE DESK

The desk serves as a central point of contact for diverse inquiries and assistance, streamlining access to information and resources for various needs.

ELDER FACILITATION DESK

The desk strives to support and improve the quality of life for elderly individuals by providing resources, advice, and services customized to their specific requirements and difficulties.
